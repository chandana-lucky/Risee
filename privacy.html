Privacy Policy - RISE

Last updated: September 2025

Welcome to RISE — the “Gamification of Completing Tasks for School Students” App. We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use RISE.

This policy outlines:

  • What information we collect and why.
  • How we store, use, and protect your data.
  • Your rights and choices regarding your personal data.
  • Contact details for any concerns or feedback.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you sign up or use RISE, we may collect the following details:

  • Full Name
  • Username (for app login)
  • Email address (for account verification)
  • School name or grade (optional)
  • Age group (for personalized experience)

1.2 Usage and Performance Data

  • Device information (OS version, device type)
  • App usage patterns (tasks created, completed)
  • Achievements and progress logs
  • Crash reports for improving app stability

1.3 Gamification Data

  • Task completion history (to grow your virtual plant companion)
  • Streak data and milestones
  • Level progress and achievements

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us provide a motivating and gamified experience. We use it to:

  • Personalize your dashboard and plant growth progress.
  • Store and display completed tasks, achievements, and streaks.
  • Improve user experience through analytics and updates.
  • Send helpful reminders, notifications, and motivational messages.
  • Maintain data integrity and prevent unauthorized access.

3. Data Security

We prioritize your privacy and employ various measures to protect it:

  • Secure authentication (password encryption).
  • Protected cloud database storage.
  • Restricted access to sensitive data.
  • Regular backups and system updates.

4. Your Rights

As a RISE user, you can:

  • Access and modify your profile information.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Review your progress and achievement records anytime.
  • Opt out of receiving promotional notifications.

5. Third-Party Services

RISE may use trusted third-party services for secure operations:

  • Firebase (authentication and notifications)
  • Google Analytics (performance and usage insights)
  • Cloud Database (for secure data storage)

All partners comply with relevant data protection standards.

6. Children’s Privacy

RISE is designed specifically for students aged 10–17. We ensure a child-friendly and safe environment. If any data from users below this age group is found, it will be removed immediately.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be revised periodically to reflect improvements or changes in our app. Updated versions will be posted in-app and on our official website.
